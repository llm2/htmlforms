Description of the Tutorial

Please provide a description of the proposed tutorial. The description should be modeled on the course descriptions in the Gallatin course listing booklet. (For more details about the proposal, please see the section on "Designing a Tutorial.") The description should be approximately 250 words, and the theme, content and scope of the project should be clearly stated.

Work to be Evaluated by the Instructor

Work to be Evaluated by the Instructor: Assignments should also be comparable to a similar classroom course (e.g., interdisciplinary seminars require approximately 20 to 30 pages of writing over the course of the semester). Be more specific than "papers," "essays," or "portfolio": indicate how many papers, what length they will be, whether research will be involved, when they will be due, etc.

Syllabus

A schedule of meetings should be produced to specify the topics and readings to be covered at each session, as well the expected deadlines for assignments. Students can revise and adopt their syllabus with their instructor once the semester begins.
