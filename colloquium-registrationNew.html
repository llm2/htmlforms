Student Information

Committee Information

The colloquium committee consists of your adviser and two other faculty members. At least one member of your committee must be a full-time Gallatin faculty member. If you are having difficulty finding faculty members for you committee, please contact the senior class adviser.

Date and Time of Colloquium

Based on space availability colloquia are generally scheduled in one of our small conference rooms, which are equipped to display media on a large wall-mounted flat-screen monitor with speakers from the internet, DVD, flash drive through a MacMini, or directly from your laptop--if you bring the cables. Alternately, colloquia are scheduled in the office of the student's adviser or the full-time Gallatin faculty member of the committee. Please note below any media/tech or communication needs you have that may need further accommodation.
