The Adviser’s Role

  • The Committee on Individualized Studies will assume that your approval of an advisee’s independent study proposal signifies genuine support for the project. Therefore, if you have questions about the validity of the study, please do not automatically sign or approve the proposal form.  Rather, work with the student until she or he presents a study you can approve.
  • Please advise your student to find an alternate classroom course in case the proposal is not approved.

Proposal Review and Approval

  1. Review your advisee’s proposal (below) and the attachment of required proposal components which include:

    • a completed syllabus of dates and work required
