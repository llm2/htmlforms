The Instructor’s Role:

  1. After agreeing to work with the student you should review the appropriate Independent Study Proposal Guidelines for detailed information on designing an independent study including the proposal components, the number of credits and meeting hours.
    • Undergraduate Independent Study Proposal Guidelines
    • Graduate Independent Study Proposal Guidelines
  2. You should also review the Instructor Guidelines for information that is specific to your role as the instructor of a Gallatin Independent Study.
    • Undergraduate Independent Study Instructor Guidelines
    • Graduate Independent Study Instructor Guidelines
    • Student’s will be notified of approval and instructed to register before classes begin for the semester.  Do not begin working with a student until the student is officially registered for the independent study.   Before your first meeting with the student, confirm registration by checking your class roster in the Faculty Center on Albert.   If you have questions, concerns or need assistance with confirming the student’s registration status, contact Kathe Ann Joseph.

Shortly after classes begin Gallatin will send you an email with registration, payment and grading information.  Feel free to contact Kathe Ann Joseph if you have questions before then.

Proposal Review and Approval:

  1. Review your student’s proposal (below) and the required proposal components which include:
    • the description of the study
    • a booklist
    • a clearly explained “work to be evaluated” (a minimum of 20- 25 pages of written work assigned).
    • a completed syllabus of dates and work required

    2. Please review this proposal completely before sending your approval.

  2. Once you are ready to approve this proposal, please reply to this email to submit your approval before the appropriate deadline.

    Students were informed to submit this proposal to you with enough advance notice for you to be able to approve this by the following deadline:

    Undergraduate Student Proposal Submission Deadline

    Fall proposal – May 1*
    Spring proposal – December 1*
    Summer proposal – May 1*

    *If this date falls on a Saturday or Sunday, the deadline will be extended to the Monday following the deadline.

    Graduate Student Proposal Submission Deadline

    Fall or Spring proposal – last day of the first week of classes
    Summer Proposal – first day of summer session classes
