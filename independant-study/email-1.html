Thank you for submitting your Independent Study Proposal. Because you must take additional action before your independent study proposal can be approved, please read this entire message to make sure your proposal is not denied.

  1. Be sure that you have included all of the required components with your proposal submission including:
    • a clearly explained “Work to be Evaluated”
    • a minimum of 20-25 pages of written work assigned 
    • a completed syllabus of dates and work required
  2. Adviser and Grading Instructor Approval:

    3. Your Independent Study proposal has been sent to your adviser and grading instructor for approval using the email addresses you listed on the proposal form.  Your adviser and grading instructor will forward approval directly to the Faculty  Committee on Individualized Studies.  Follow up with them before the proposal submission deadline to make sure they have received your proposal form and have sent their approval to Gallatin.

    ** If you did not discuss this project with them prior to submitting this form, your proposal may not be approved by one or both of them.  

  3. Final Approval:

    4. The Gallatin Faculty Committee on Individualized Studies reviews all Independent Study proposals for final approval. 

    Your proposal will not be reviewed for final approval until the committee receives:

    1. Your proposal and all of the required components by the appropriate submission deadline
    2. approval from your adviser
    3. approval from your grading instructor

    After your proposal has been reviewed, you will be notified of the committee’s decision via email.  Look out for the email as you might be asked to provide additional materials or you might receive codes to register on Albert.

    The Faculty Committee on Individualized Studies plans to review and respond to all students approximately two weeks after the submission deadline. Proposals submitted prior to the deadline may be reviewed sooner.

  4. Registration:

    5. If your proposal is approved, you will receive an email with registration codes.  Log in to Albert and register for your independent study.  Please note that students must register for their independent study prior to the first meeting with the instructor.  Your grading instructor will not work with you until your enrollment is confirmed on Albert.  If you run into an issue with registration please contact Kathe Ann Joseph as soon as possible.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact KatheAnn Joseph (katheann.joseph@nyu.edu) at Gallatin.

Sincerely,

Faculty Committee on Individualized Studies
