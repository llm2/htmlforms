Letter of Recommendation

Please submit a letter of recommendation from a faculty member or primary academic advisor that speaks of your academic standing and ability to study away in an intensive course. Letters may be mailed or hand delivered to the address above, or emailed to gallatin.global@nyu.edu.

Interview

Once we have received your application, personal statement, transcript and letter of recommendation, the Gallatin Office of Global Programs will contact you to arrange an interview. Please note that due to limited space available in the programs, not all applicants may be interviewed.
