Registration and Program Restrictions

  1. Internships: Undergraduate students may enroll in as many as (but no more than) 24 total internship units during their undergraduate course of study, and as many as (but no more than) four internship units per semester.
  2. Business courses: Due to business school accreditation restrictions, no more than 31 credits in business courses can count towards the Gallatin degree. This includes courses in the Stern School, the McGhee Division in the School of Professional Studies, and those transferred from other colleges and universities.
  3. Private Lessons: Undergraduate students may not take more than 24 credits in private lessons during their studies at the Gallatin School. Included in this total will be units earned from Gallatin Private Lessons (INDIV-UG 1701) as well as units earned from Steinhardt music courses noted as “individual instruction in the performing arts” (e.g., Participation in NYU Orchestra, Private Vocal Training, etc.).
  4. Liberal Studies Program (LSP) Courses: After matriculation at Gallatin students are not permitted to receive degree credit for LSP courses. The subject area code for these courses contains “- UF”
  5. School of Professional Studies Restriction (SPS): Gallatin students are not permitted to receive degree credit for certain courses in SPS. The subject area code for these courses contains “- UC” Please review the list of the SPS courses that Gallatin students can take for credit.

To Approve the Course Selections Below:
Please reply to this email to submit your approval. Students will be cleared according to the following unit/credit limits:

  • Fall/Spring – 18 units/credits per semester
  • Six-week summer session – 8 units/credits per semester
  • January term -4 units/credits in the three-week term

Approval above the unit/credit limit:
With the exception of January term, students may receive approval to register above the unit/credit limit for a particular term. If you are approving your advisee to register above the unit/credit limit, please specify the number of units/credits and note the following:

  • First-year students are not permitted to register for more than 18 units/credits in their first semester of study.
  • A petition is also required if a student (1) requests approval above 20 units/credits for fall/spring, (2) does not have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and/or (3) has outstanding incomplete grades.

If You Cannot Approve the Course Selections Below:
Please reply to your advisee with the recommended changes and have him/her resubmit a revised Plan of Study form.

If you have other questions or concerns, please e-mail us at studentservices.gallatin@nyu.edu.
